The Best Movies According to Jed

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Terminal

One of my all time favorites. Not very well known, it's about a sweet story of a man getting stranded at an airport terminal.

Interstellar

A great space movie that is scientifically accurate, and has cool depictions of planets, space, and time.

Whiplash

Insane movie about a drummer who aims to please a never satisfied professor.
